Collaborative Filtering

Collaborative filtering is based on the assumption that people who agreed in the past will agree in the future, and that they will like similar kinds of items as they liked in the past. The motivation for this is people often get the best recommendations from someone with tastes similar to themselves.

Content Based Filtering

Content-based filtering methods are based on the description of the item and the profile of the user’s preferences. These methods are best suited to situations where there is known data on an item (name, location, description, and so on ), but not on the user.
